About Sun Crew Energy

Learn who we are, what drives us, and why hundreds of clients across Egypt trust us with their solar energy projects.

Our Story

Built on Expertise, Driven by Purpose

Sun Crew Energy was founded by a team of engineers and business professionals with proven track records in large-scale EPC projects and B2B client engagement. Our combined background spans mechanical engineering, construction management, QA/QC, and pre-sales solution architecture.

We serve commercial, industrial, and hospitality clients across Egypt — from factories and warehouses to hotels, resorts, and large commercial campuses. Our EPC approach ensures every project is delivered with engineering rigour, quality-sourced equipment, and professional installation from start to finish.

We believe solar energy is not just an investment — it's a step towards a more sustainable and independent future. That belief is at the heart of everything we do.
