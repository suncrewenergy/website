How It Works

From First Survey to Final Handover

01

Send Inquiry

Contact us through WhatsApp, phone, or the inquiry form and share your project type, location, and energy needs.

02

Initial Consultation

Our team reviews your requirements, explains the best options, and confirms the information needed for assessment.

03

Site Assessment

We assess the roof, land, electrical setup, consumption profile, shading conditions, and installation feasibility.

04

Technical Study

Our engineers prepare system sizing, layout direction, equipment selection, and expected solar production.

05

Proposal & Quotation

You receive a clear proposal with scope, specifications, pricing, project timeline, and recommended solution.

06

Procurement & Planning

We source the required equipment, prepare execution plans, and coordinate project logistics before installation.

07

Installation & Testing

Our crew installs the system, completes wiring and mounting, then tests performance and safety before operation.

08

Handover & Support

We hand over the system with guidance, documentation, and maintenance options for long-term performance.

About Us

Who Is Sun Crew Energy?

Sun Crew Energy is a specialised solar energy company committed to delivering clean, reliable, and cost-effective solar EPC solutions for commercial, industrial, and hospitality clients across Egypt.

Founded by engineers with deep EPC and international project experience, our mission is to help businesses — from factories and warehouses to hotels and large commercial campuses — harness the power of the sun, cut energy costs, and build long-term energy independence.
